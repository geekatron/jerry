
DOMAIN PLAYBOOKS (CLI / vi)

USAGE:
- Frame with 5W1H
- Classify: Simple / Complicated / Complex / Chaotic
- Apply domain loop
- Verify + Learn

--- SOFTWARE ---
Triage->Diagnose->Fix->Verify->Learn
- Stabilize users first (rollback/flag/rate-limit)
- Hypothesis-driven debugging (logs/metrics/traces)
- Blameless postmortem, action items

--- SECURITY ---
Prepare->Detect/Analyze->Contain->Eradicate->Recover->Post-incident
- Preserve evidence
- Contain fast; reduce attacker movement
- Rotate secrets, patch, monitor relapse

--- SCIENCE ---
Observe->Hypothesize (competing)->Design test->Run->Analyze->Replicate->Revise
- Define falsification criteria
- Separate measurement error vs model error

--- SKILLS ACQUISITION ---
Define standard->Decompose->Pick 1 subskill->Deliberate practice->Measure->Integrate->Review
- Short reps + fast feedback
- Avoid skipping stages

--- COOKING ---
Goal->Identify CCPs (time/temp/contamination)->Measure->Adjust 1 variable->Document
- Safety first (temps, danger zone, cooling rules)
- Treat recipe as spec (version it)
