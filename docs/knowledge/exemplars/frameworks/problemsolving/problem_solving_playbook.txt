   - Who?
   - What?
   - Where?
   - When?
   - Why?
   - How?

2. CLASSIFY
   - Simple
   - Complicated
   - Complex
   - Chaotic

3. DIAGNOSE
   - Linear? → 5 Whys
   - Multi? → Fishbone
   - Unknown? → Hypothesis

4. IDEATE
   - Generate options
   - No judgement

5. DECIDE
   - Risk
   - Cost
   - Time
   - Reversibility

6. ACT
   - Execute
   - Monitor

7. VERIFY
   - Did it work?

8. LEARN
   - Document
   - Prevent recurrence
