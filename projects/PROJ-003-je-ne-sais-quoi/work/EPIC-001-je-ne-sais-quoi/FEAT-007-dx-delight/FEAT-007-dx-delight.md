## Summary

Small touches that make the difference between a tool you use and a tool you love. Session start personality. Progress celebrations. The feeling of working WITH a companion, not UNDER a supervisor.

**Value Proposition:**
- Transforms developer experience from compliance to companionship
- Celebration moments reinforce positive behavior
- Session personality creates emotional connection
