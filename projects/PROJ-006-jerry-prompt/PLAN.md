## Objective

Research and identify the most effective prompt patterns, structures, and techniques for working with the Jerry framework. Produce actionable guidance that improves prompt quality and Jerry's output consistency.

## Scope

- Prompt patterns that work well with Jerry's skill system
- Effective ways to invoke skills, agents, and workflows
- Anti-patterns that degrade Jerry's performance
- Prompt templates for common Jerry tasks
