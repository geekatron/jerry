## Summary

Technical review of research synthesis for rigor, completeness, and citation integrity. Output via nse-reviewer-001.

## Problem Statement

Research synthesis requires independent technical review before content production phase to catch methodological gaps and citation issues.

## Business Value

Prevents content production from proceeding with flawed synthesis, protecting publication quality per R-004 and R-005.

## Technical Approach
